Green-Blitz

GreenBlitz is the official robotics team of the Hakfar Hayarok high school. We took part in the FIRST FRC competition for the first time in the 2013 season.

What is FRC?

The varsity Sport for the Mind FRC combines the excitement of sport with the rigors of science and technology. Under strict rules, limited resources, and time limits, teams of 25 students or more are challenged to raise funds, design a team "brand," hone teamwork skills, and build and program robots to perform prescribed tasks against a field of competitors. The Team

CEO
Manage the team, make sure they follow the schedule, and solve issues within the team. Furthermore, they pursue ways to lift the team's morale and make sure the FRC experience is fun and enriching.
Software
Create a program for the robot's mechanisms and electrical controllers, for the purpose of making it functional and responsive for both the autonomous and the teleoperated periods.
Design
Design a 3D model of the robot using a SolidWorks software, in order to finalize the the robot's structure, as well as to test the physical stability of the robot.
Systems
Produce an electrical controller system, as well as a pneumatic system, which are connected to the robot's mechanisms in order to for them to fully operate.
Media
Produce videos and images that document major events in the team. In addition, manage the team's image and social network pages.
Chairmen
Manage the team's social projects, establish projects for the purpose of contributing to the community, with an emphasis on spreading technology as well as our team's and FIRST's vision.
Strategy
In charge of devising a winning tactic, as well as managing scouting for the purpose of creating the best alliance.
Construction
Designs and builds the robot and its mechanisms from scratch, using raw materials and various tools.
Mentors
Enrich the team members with their knowledge and experience, as well as provide guidance through the year.
